Why Privacy Settings Matter

Privacy settings help you control who sees your data ‚Äî like your posts, location, and profile info. Every app has its own settings, and students should learn how to use them.

Instagram

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy
  • Turn on Private Account so only approved followers can see your posts
  • Block or restrict users who send unwanted messages
  • Limit who can comment or tag you

YouTube

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy
  • Hide your subscriptions and liked videos
  • Turn off comments on your videos if needed
  • Use Restricted Mode to block unsafe content

Google Account

  • Visit myaccount.google.com
  • Check Privacy Checkup to review what data is being shared
  • Turn off location history and ad personalization
  • Use strong passwords and enable 2-step verification

Tips for Students

Review your settings every month. Apps update often, and new features may affect your privacy. Never share passwords with friends, and avoid logging in on public devices.

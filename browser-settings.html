Why Browser Settings Matter

Your browser is your gateway to the internet. If it‚Äôs not secure, you can be exposed to scams, trackers, and unsafe content. Setting up your browser properly helps protect your data and your device.

Google Chrome

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy and Security
  • Enable Safe Browsing (Enhanced Protection)

  • Block third-party cookies and pop-ups
  • Clear browsing data regularly

Microsoft Edge

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Privacy, Search, and Services
  • Choose Strict Tracking Prevention

Mobile Browsers

  • Use incognito/private mode when needed
  • Disable location access for browsers
  • Install ad blockers or parental control apps
  • Update your browser regularly

Can You Spot the Scam?

Scenario 1: You get a message saying “🎁 You’ve won ₹10,000! Click here to claim your prize.”

Scenario 2: Your school sends an email asking you to update your password using the school website.

Scenario 3: A stranger sends you a friend request and says “I’m your cousin from Delhi. Let’s chat!”

Scenario 4: A pop-up says “Your device is infected! Download this app now!”

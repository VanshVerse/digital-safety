What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is the act of hurting someone through digital platforms like social media, messaging apps, or online games. It includes sending mean messages, spreading rumors, sharing embarrassing photos, or creating fake profiles to insult or harass someone.

For students, understanding cyberbullying is very important. As we spend more time online for studies and entertainment, we must learn how to behave responsibly and respectfully. Cyberbullying can happen anytime, anywhere ‚Äî even outside school hours.

  • Sending threatening or abusive messages on WhatsApp
